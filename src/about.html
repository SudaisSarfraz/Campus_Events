About Campus Events

Campus Events is an initiative by University Of Cental Punjab to bring all academic, cultural, and co-curricular happenings under one digital roof. Our goal is to make it easier for students, staff, and visitors to stay informed and engaged with the vibrant life on campus.

Our Mission

To create a connected and dynamic university experience by leveraging technology to promote collaboration, awareness, and community participation in all campus activities.

Project Purpose

The purpose of this project is to design a centralized platform that allows users to explore upcoming events, register for activities, view schedules, and relive memories through photo galleries — all in one place. This project aims to streamline event management and enhance communication between students and organizers.
